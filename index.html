These are some self-study modules I've done. These are things I'm curious about, things that keep me up at night, and things I want to make you curious about, too. I'm excited to share what I've learned with you.

wayfinding and augmented reality

I got curious about how we interface with the built environment and found an opportunity to change those interactions with up-and-coming augmented reality interaction patterns.

inclusion

I'd watched some talks, seen some documentation, and read some posts on the matter, but I wanted to aggregate my findings into best practices that could be applied to some apps that we interact with on a daily basis.

chatbots

Chatbots are the latest rage in design and tech trends. I dig into "What does it mean to design a conversational interface?", and some examples of some good ones.

writing for interfaces

We typically neglect writing as a part of designing interfaces, but good written content can make all the difference. In this module, I dive into what it means to write good interface content and examples of companies who do it well.
